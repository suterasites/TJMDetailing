## Anti-pattern flagged

The home page LocalBusiness schema declares:

```
"aggregateRating": {
  "ratingValue": "5.0",
  "reviewCount": "100",
  "bestRating": "5"
}
```

The CRM source for this claim is "Reviews: 100% 5-Star" (in `CRM/clients/TJM Detailing/profile.md`) - that is a *percentage* of reviews being 5-star, not 100 individual reviews. The schema is misrepresenting a percentage as a count. Per SOP, remove until James confirms a real count from a single platform (Google Business Profile or Facebook) at ≥ 10 reviews.

Body copy still says "100% 5-Star Reviews" / "Rated 5.0 on Google" - that phrasing is defensible as a *percentage* (every review is 5-star). Leaving body copy alone for now; James to verify if/when content is rewritten in Phase 4.
