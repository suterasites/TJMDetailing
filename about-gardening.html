Our Story

BUILT ON
STANDARDS

TJM Detailing started with one idea: do the job properly, every single time. That same commitment to quality we bring to our auto detailing extends to every yard and garden we maintain.

Based in Melbourne's western suburbs, our garden maintenance service covers everything from regular lawn mowing and edging to hedge trimming, weeding, and full yard clean-ups. We bring all the equipment and leave your property looking its best.

We operate on weekends only - Saturday and Sunday, 8 AM to 5 PM - which means we're available exactly when it's most convenient for you. Every job is done with care, using the right tools, and backed by full insurance for your peace of mind.
