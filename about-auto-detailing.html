Our Story

BUILT ON
STANDARDS

TJM Detailing started with one idea: do the job properly, every single time. No shortcuts, no rushing, no cutting corners to fit in an extra booking.

Based in Melbourne's western suburbs, we're a mobile detailing service that comes to you. Whether it's at your home, your office, or wherever your vehicle is parked - we bring everything we need and leave your car looking like it just rolled off the lot.

We operate on weekends only - Saturday and Sunday, 8 AM to 5 PM - which means we're available exactly when it's most convenient for you. Every detail is done by hand, with professional-grade products, and backed by full insurance for your peace of mind.

How It Works

OUR
PROCESS

From booking to the final wipe-down, here's what a TJM detail looks like. Simple, professional, and built around your schedule.

Book Your Detail
01

Call & Book

Give us a call on 0447 418 866 to lock in your weekend booking. We'll discuss your vehicle, the package you're after, and confirm the details.

02

We Come to You

On your booked day, we arrive at your location with all equipment and products ready to go. No need to drive anywhere or rearrange your day.

03

Vehicle Inspection

We inspect your vehicle before starting to identify any areas that need special attention - stains, scratches, heavy soiling, or anything that might affect pricing.

04

The Detail

We get to work. Depending on your chosen package, this could be a focused interior clean, a full exterior hand wash, or a complete top-to-bottom detail with steam cleaning and UV protection.

05

Final Walk-Through

Once we're done, we walk you through the results. We don't pack up until you're completely satisfied. That's the standard - every job, every time.
