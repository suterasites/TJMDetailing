Our Story

YOUR YARD,
OUR PRIDE

TJM Detailing isn't just about vehicles - we bring the same hands-on care and attention to your outdoor spaces. A tidy yard makes a real difference, and we take pride in making yours look its best.

Based in Melbourne's western suburbs, we offer a full range of gardening and yard maintenance services. From regular lawn mowing to hedge trimming, weeding, and full yard clean-ups - we handle it all so you don't have to.

We operate on weekends only - Saturday and Sunday, 8 AM to 5 PM - which means your yard gets sorted when it suits your schedule. Fully insured and committed to quality work, every single visit.

What We Offer

OUR SERVICES

From regular lawn mowing to full yard overhauls - we keep your outdoor spaces looking sharp. Pricing varies by property size and scope of work.

Lawn Mowing

A clean, even cut across your entire lawn. We leave your grass looking neat and well-maintained, with clippings collected or mulched to your preference.

Edging & Whipper Snipping

Crisp, defined edges along driveways, paths, and garden beds. We detail those hard-to-reach spots that make the whole yard look polished.

Hedge Trimming

Neat, shaped hedges that frame your property properly. We trim to your preferred shape and height, keeping everything looking tidy and well-kept.

Weeding Garden Beds

Thorough weeding of all garden beds. We remove weeds by the root so your garden stays cleaner for longer and your plants have room to thrive.

Green Waste Removal

All green waste collected and removed from your property. No piles left behind - we take everything with us so your yard is clean when we leave.

General Yard Tidy

A complete tidy-up of your outdoor space. Clearing leaves, debris, and general mess - everything gets sorted so your property looks its best.

Clearing Leaves & Debris

Thorough clearing of all outdoor areas - driveways, paths, gutters, and garden beds. We make sure every surface is free of fallen leaves, branches, and built-up debris.

Pricing varies by property size and scope of work. Call us for a free, no-obligation quote.
